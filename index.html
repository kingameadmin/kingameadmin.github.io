PROOF OF STAKING




Proof of stake is a platform that allows participating users to lock a certain quantity of KIN tokens to receive mining rewards from them.
This amount is locked in Blockchain nodes for an exact period; at the same time, the reward will be based on the user's effort, including the KIN token earnings and stake duration.
